ReferNow

Working people, tired of getting 100+ DMs almost everyday in your LinkedIn for referral requests?
Students, tired of sending cold messages and mails to the employees working in your favourite company?
Cut that hassle and step onto ReferNow where employees can find students to refer to and where students can find the availability of referrals. Employees can sort students according to their requirements for the job posting and refer the students and increase the chances of getting referred. Students can see the available referrals and apply accordingly.
